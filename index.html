What is DailyLift?

DailyLift is a content creation toolkit designed for wellness, health, and lifestyle creators who want to produce professional short-form videos without a production team.

Our pipeline handles everything from script to publish: AI narration, stock footage curation, subtitle generation, and multi-platform distribution.

TikTok Integration

DailyLift integrates with TikTok's Content Posting API to allow creators to upload their produced wellness videos directly to TikTok. This reduces the manual steps in a creator's publishing workflow — produce once, publish everywhere.

The integration uses TikTok's official OAuth flow and respects all platform guidelines. Creators authenticate their own TikTok account and control what gets published.
